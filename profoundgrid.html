PROFOUNDGRID

A responsive grid system for fixed and fluid layouts.Built in SCSS,It gives you flexibility and full control.

Responsive

Make the grid fluid or fixed and add per-medialayouts as needed.Change the column and gutter widths and remove or add columns as needed.

Semantic

The grid keeps your markup semantic and works withoutany "grid_x" classes.Unless you want to use them,in which case they are included.

Precise

Profound grid uses negative margins to calculate columns.Unlike with other grid systems,fluid layouts will look exactly thesame in every browser.
